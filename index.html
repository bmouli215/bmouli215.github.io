My Philosophy of Education

Education is not the filling of a vessel, but the kindling of a flame. I believe in shaping students into innovators, leaders, and responsible citizens who carry both technical expertise and strong values. Through knowledge, curiosity, and collaboration, we build not just successful careers, but a stronger society.
