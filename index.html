About

I am a Professor & Head of the Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering at QIS College of Engineering & Technology, Ongole. My research work focuses on power electronics, FPGA-based control for drives and FACTS devices, renewable energy integration and EV technologies.

Research & Projects

Selected funded projects

  • SERB (ECR) project on FPGA estimators for induction motor parameter estimation. (₹24,15,600).
  • AICTE IDEA Labs (Coordinator) — IDEA LAB funding (₹1,02,00,000).
  • FIST / DST improvement grants and MODROBS projects (details in CV).

Research interests

Power Electronics, FACTS, FPGA controllers, EV energy management, microgrid integration, PV inverters and high-gain converters.
